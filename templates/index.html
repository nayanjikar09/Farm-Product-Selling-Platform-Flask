About Us

This web-based application is a complete package for promoting organic farming and consumption. It is designed to benefit farmers by providing insights for sustainable organic practices and empower consumers and retailers to buy organic products directly from farmers. Key features include:

  • Crop Prediction: Using past agricultural data, weather conditions, and soil properties, the application predicts suitable organic crops to grow, ensuring optimal yield and sustainability .
  • Organic Farming Guidance: It offers recommendations on organic fertilizers and pest control methods based on land conditions and atmospheric data, promoting eco-friendly farming practices.
  • Marketplace for Organic Products: The platform connects organic farmers directly with retailers and consumers, eliminating middlemen and ensuring fair pricing for farmers while providing high-quality organic products to buyers .
  • Consumer Focus: Retailers and consumers can browse and purchase fresh, organic products with ease, fostering a healthier lifestyle and supporting organic agriculture.
